Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a practice session?

Tap the "Practice" tab, choose your preferred difficulty level and operations, set the number of questions and time limit, then tap "Start Practice".

What difficulty levels are available?

MathDrill offers three difficulty levels: Easy (numbers up to 20), Medium (numbers up to 100), and Hard (numbers up to 500). You can switch levels anytime before starting a session.

Can I practice specific operations only?

Yes! Before starting a session, tap the operation buttons (+, -, x, /) to select which ones you want to include. You must select at least one operation.

How does the Wrong Book work?

Whenever you answer a question incorrectly, it is automatically saved to your Wrong Book. You can review these problems later in the "Wrong Book" tab and clear them at any time.

How is my accuracy calculated?

Your accuracy is calculated across all completed practice sessions as the percentage of correctly answered questions out of the total questions attempted.

Is my data backed up?

MathDrill stores all data locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled for your device, your data will be included in your encrypted backup. We do not operate any cloud servers.

How do I reset all my data?

Go to the "Settings" tab and tap "Reset All Data". This will permanently delete all practice records and wrong answer history. This action cannot be undone.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! MathDrill works entirely offline. No internet connection is required to practice math or view your statistics.
